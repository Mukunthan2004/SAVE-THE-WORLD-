We are a group to save this world

Climate change is one of the most pressing issues facing our planet today. With rising temperatures, melting ice caps, and more extreme weather events, the need for action is urgent. Fortunately, there are a number of ways in which we can work towards a climate safer future.


Climate Change

The effects of climate change are already being felt around the world, with more intense heat waves, droughts, wildfires, and hurricanes affecting communities and ecosystems. These impacts are expected to become even more severe in the coming decades, with the potential for widespread food and water shortages, displacement of millions of people, and damage to infrastructure and economies.

Deforestation Causes

Between 1990 and 2020, the world lost 420 million hectares of forest, an area larger than the size of South Africa. In 2020 alone, an estimated 10.1 million hectares of forest were lost worldwide, equivalent to one football pitch every 2 seconds. The countries with the highest rates of deforestation in recent years include Brazil, Indonesia, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.
